Dom Selectors

document.getElementById ('something')

  • getElementsByTagName
  • getElementsByClassName
  • getElementById
  • querySelector
  • querySelectorAll

With querySelector we target what we want like document.querySelector('.divWithClass elementInDiv')
If we use querySelector on a class/tag or anything what have more than one exists, only return the first it finds. In this case use querySelectorAll .
